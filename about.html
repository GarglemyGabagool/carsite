ABOUT

A little about me and my background,

My name's Nick, I was born on 1995 in Birmingham, AL. I grew up in Center Point which is an awful little run down town north East of Birmingham, past Roebuck. I've loved tech and computers my whole life, I practically grew up on them. I even learned how to build them when I was around 12 years old.

My first real job was working at Publix Supermarkets, where I quickly got up to Assistant Department Manager, before realizing that the environment was not what I really wanted to live with for the rest of my life. So I ended up looking into ways to move careers to something closer to my natural tech interests.
