Cars That I've Personally Owned

Galant

Technical Specifications

Model Year: 1999

Engine: It had one

Horsepower: Great question

Torque: No idea

0-60: Over a period of time

Color: White

My Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Cheap, cost me only 700 dollars
  • Was my first car, gave me a way to get from point A to point B

Cons

  • Serpentine belt would squeal like a pig in pain
  • Was in bad condition inside and out, front bumper was hanging off
  • It only lasted 3 months before the air compressor died
  • Not really pretty or interesting in any way

Mustang Ecoboost

Technical Specifications

Model Year: 2018

Engine: 2.0L 4-Cylinder

Horsepower: 310 HP

Torque: 350 lb-ft

0-60: 5.5 seconds

Color: Ingot Silver

My Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Was my first 'real' car, financed and everything
  • A lot nicer than the Galant, inside and out
  • More interesting to drive than the Galant
  • Taught me a bunch of basic car maintenance like oil changes and air filter changes
  • Decent cargo space, rear seats folded down
  • Good gas mileage for a 'muscle' car
  • Had creature comforts like an infotainment system with Android Auto/Carplay

Cons

  • Subpar handling, back liked to break free
  • Not super quick in the grand scheme of things
  • Pretty weak exhaust note, not the V8
  • Relatively large for a two-door coupe
  • 10-speed automatic started having awfully harsh shifts, would shake the car

3LT Corvette Z51

Technical Specifications

Model Year: 2015

Engine: 6.2L 8-Cylinder

Horsepower: 460 HP

Torque: 465 lb-ft

0-60: 3.7 seconds

Color: Silver Metallic

My Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Absolutely beautiful car, I loved the red interior and the silver/black/red together
  • Incredibly fast with great handling
  • Ton of space in the trunk and easy to utilize thanks to having a hatchback
  • Surprisingly good fuel economy for an 8-cylinder sports car
  • Hatchback trunk makes putting stuff in the cargo compartment easy
  • Incredible exhaust note
  • It appreciated in value while I owned it

Cons

  • The targa top leaked on the driver's side whenever it rained
  • Interior quality was a mixed bag, lots of cheap plastic
  • Had no wireless Carplay or Android Auto
  • No safety features like blind spot monitoring in a car with poor visibility
  • No parking sensors or front parking camera

Civic Sport Touring

Technical Specifications

Model Year: 2022

Engine: 1.5L 4-Cylinder

Horsepower: 180 HP

Torque: 177 lb-ft

0-60: 7.7 seconds

Color: Lunar Silver Metallic

My Pros and Cons

Pros

  • First new car that I purchased
  • Relatively cheap, especially compared to the Corvette or Supra
  • Had a good amount of interior space
  • One of the most beautiful hatchbacks around this price point on the market
  • Good fuel economy

Cons

  • Awful amounts of road noise on basically every road
  • Wireless Carplay would glitch out constantly, had to constantly pair my phone back to the car
  • Wireless charging pad was useless, phone drain was faster than the charge
  • Infotainment screen was pretty terrible
  • Car was VERY slow and although it was kind of zippy but not terribly interesting to drive

A91 Supra

Technical Specifications

Model Year: 2021

Engine: 3.0 L 6-Cylinder

Horsepower: 382 HP

Torque: 368 lb-ft

0-60: 3.9 seconds

Color: Refraction Blue

My Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Absolutely beautiful car, very unique styling and turns heads everywhere
  • The blue color is incredible
  • Very small, easy to park
  • Good fuel economy for a sports car
  • Hatchback trunk makes putting stuff in the cargo compartment easy
  • Very fast car with great handling
  • Has wireless Apple Carplay
  • Great infotainment system borrowed from BMW
  • Very good chassis/powertrain for doing mods on

Cons

  • Wind buffeting when you roll down the windows
  • Like the corvette, interior material quality is a mixed bag
  • Wireless charging pad isn't as bad as the Civic but it's not much better
  • OEM wheels are pretty ugly
  • No button to open the trunk from the outside, have to use keyfob
